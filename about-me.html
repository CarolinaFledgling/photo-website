photographer

Nigel Waldron shooting under the name Photo2View has been working as a photographer for close to two decades. He has covered major events internationally, and one of his annual favorites is the Nobel Peace Prize Ceremony, where he has been shooting images over the last decade.

The art of photography brings continues learning and positive challenges for Nigel through creative discussions and close cooperation with the client. Together with the client Nigel aims for mutual inspiration in order to ensure capture of the desired image. His images have been published in international media, ranging from literature to such media as Sport Ilustrated to the New York Times. Nigel is at your service, no assignment is too little or too big - together we will make it happen!
